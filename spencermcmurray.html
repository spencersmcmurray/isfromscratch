My Hometown

My hometown is Abingdon Virginia. It is a small town in the Appalachian Mountains in Southwest Virginia. I moved away when I was 15 but my family returned a few years later and I couldn't be more grateful. The relationships I made in my small town have followed me wherever I go and even though I may not end up living there with my own family, I have intentions to give back to the place that raised me and still gives me the motivation to keep on going, no matter where I am. I put a picture below so you can see what you're missing out on.



My home county has severe poverty and one of the ways I want to give back is by investing in the community and helping kids get a better education and stay in school. Kids often drop out of school due to the fact that they have to work to support their families. Often times, this leads to high crime, high drug trafficking, and one of the highest opioid death rates in the entire United States.
